Demographics of Accra

Accra is the second largest city in Ghana.

Population of Accra is about five million four hundred and fifty five thousand people.

Interesting facts about the city

  • Kwame Nkrumah Mausoleum: The Kwame Nkrumah Mausoleum and Memorial Park stands as a tribute to Ghana's first president, Kwame Nkrumah, a pivotal figure in the country's struggle for independence. Visitors can explore exhibits chronicling his life and legacy.
  • Labadi Beach: Labadi Beach, also known as La Pleasure Beach, is a popular seaside destination where locals and tourists alike gather to relax, swim, and enjoy various recreational activities. The beach comes alive with music, food vendors, and lively atmosphere, especially during weekends and holidays.
  • Independence Arch: The Independence Arch, located in Independence Square, symbolizes Ghana's liberation from colonial rule. It's a significant landmark where national celebrations and ceremonies take place, commemorating the country's journey to freedom and sovereignty.
